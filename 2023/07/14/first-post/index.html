First Post!

  • July 14, 2023July 15, 2023

In just one week, my adventure begins as I embark on a Study Trip organized by my study association. I feel incredibly lucky to be one of the 31 students chosen for this trip across Asia. Our journey will span three weeks, taking us to Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, and Bandung.

During our trip, we’ll have the opportunity to explore fascinating destinations, visit universities and businesses, and immerse ourselves in different cultures. From the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur to the vibrant city-state of Singapore and the scenic beauty of Bandung, each location promises unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

The Study Visit Committee has meticulously planned our daily itineraries, ensuring a diverse range of activities and excursions. We’ll have the chance to delve into local cuisines, discover hidden gems, and engage with the vibrant communities we encounter along the way.
Additionally, the committee has organized lunch lectures and in-house days at various companies, which not only provide us with valuable insights but also serve as a source of income for part of the trip’s finances.

As we embark on this journey together, let’s make it a trip to remember for all the right reasons. From breathtaking sights to enriching cultural encounters, we’ll seize every moment and create lifelong memories. The countdown has begun, and I can’t wait to embark on this incredible adventure in just one week’s time!
